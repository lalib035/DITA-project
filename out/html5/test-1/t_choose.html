Choosing your Generic Container

HTML has two types of generic containers: an inline generic container (SPAN) and a block-level generic container (DIV). The generic container you choose will depend on the type of information you want to contain.

In order to choose your generic container, you will need the following:
  • Text editor
  • HTML document
  • Text selection you would like to "contain"
